Shri Swami Vivekanand Multistate Co. Credit Society Ltd. is a Multi State Co-operative society registered with the Ministry of Agriculture, Govt. Of India. Multi State Credit Co-operative Society strictly abides by the principles of Co-operation, the Multi State Co-operative Act, 2002 and its Rules.

Shri Swami Vivekanand Multistate Co. Credit Society Ltd. was formed by its founding members and is currently operational in the states of Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh and Karnataka.
